    Home

    Welcome to CAKEbox!

    The knowledge hub for the Computational Abilities Knowledge Exchange (CAKE) Project - a central place for the UK Digital Research Infrastructure (DRI) community to exchange knowledge, share best practices and reuse templates.

    Browse around, explore content tags or use the search box to quickly find what you need!

    See something missing? Want to share your own resources? Please contribute — your input helps grow the CAKE community.

    What you can find here:

    • How To Guides - curated advice, tips and best practices draw from real-world experiences across the community.
    • Examples and Templates - reusable examples to help you get started without staring at a blank page.
    • KE Working Groups - collaborate with the community and help to shape knowledge exhange across the CAKE network.

    Code of Conduct

    By using this site, you are agreeing to abide by the CAKE Code of Conduct, which outlines our commitment to respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration.
