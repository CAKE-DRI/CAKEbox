# ✨ Share a Tip

Got a useful insight, lesson learned, or piece of advice?

Community Top Tips is designed to make sharing quick and easy.

You do not need to write a full article — even a single sentence can help someone else.

---

## What makes a good tip?

Good tips are usually:

- Practical
- Short and clear
- Based on experience
- Helpful for others

---

## Examples

- "Always test hybrid meeting audio from the perspective of remote participants."
- "Silence after asking a question is not failure — give people time to think."
- "Provide workshop materials in advance whenever possible."
