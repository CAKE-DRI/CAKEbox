### **Responsible Generative AI in Biomedical Research: A practical knowledge-exchange toolkit for safe AI use around human and sensitive data**

This fellowship will identify how generative AI is being used in biomedical research, where misconceptions or uncertainty remain, 
and what support researchers and research-support staff actually need. 
We will use these findings to develop, deliver and evaluate practical training, decision tools and reusable guidance for safer and more standardised AI use across the wider DRI community.
