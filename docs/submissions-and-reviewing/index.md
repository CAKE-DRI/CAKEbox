These processes provide opportunities to showcase our work, learn from others, strengthen collaborations, and secure funding to continue doing the work we love.

Reviewing is essential to support these processes. By acting as a reviewer, you contribute to maintaining quality, fairness and integrity within the community. Reviewing also provides valuable insight into what makes a strong submission and can strengthen your own applications.

## CAKE Review Committee

The CAKE Review Committee is responsible for providing fair, respectful and constructive reviews to ensure CAKE funds are allocated appropriately and transparently.

Interested in getting involved? 

* Read our [Terms of Reference here](reviewer-terms-of-reference.md).
